# Washi 和紙 — paper · v1.0 (light)

The sepia poster as a whole interface: paper surfaces
(`#E2D6B4 · #EBE1C3 · #F3EBD5`), sumi-ink text (`#2B2117` family),
gold sunk deeper to stay readable. Two inversions keep the language:

- the paper artifact card becomes a **lacquer island** (darkness inside light)
- the terminal is **not** inverted — always a kogane-dark island `#0A0907`

Contrast guards (verified): text-gold only `#7A5F2E` (4.6:1); fills on gold
use `#7A5F2E` + bone text (5.2:1); scarlet text `#A8352B`, moss text `#4A6B38`.
