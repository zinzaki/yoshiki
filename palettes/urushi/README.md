# Urushi 漆 — second primary · v1.0

A lacquer box: gold may never be an area — only a line and a point.
Hairlines, glyphs, kanji, underlines stay gold; everything clickable
larger than ~20px is led by ivory bone (dark text on bone fills).
Gold-as-vein reads more precious than gold-as-fill.

Tokens are identical to kogane (`inherits: kogane`); only the role casting
changes. Terminal cursor becomes bone `#EDE3C4`.

Choose urushi for action-dense interfaces and long working sessions —
bone is calmer than gold as the leading light.
