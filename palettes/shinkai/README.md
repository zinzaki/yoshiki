# Shinkai 深海 — deep water · v1.0

Kogane's roles on a cold stage: the warm lacquer is replaced by deep teal
water (`#05181F · #082630 · #0C313B`, hairlines `#1C4953`). Bone, gold and
the triggers stay warm — warm light on cold water is what makes the gold
read as precious. Terminal background sinks to `#03141A`.

Pick shinkai for nights, media contexts, or simply a second darkness
without changing the language.
