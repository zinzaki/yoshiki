# Washi 和紙 — light · v1.0

The sepia poster as a whole interface: paper surfaces
(`#E2D6B4 · #EBE1C3 · #F3EBD5`), sumi-ink text (`#2B2117` family),
gold sunk deeper to stay readable — and still only gilding (frames, edges,
text), never a fill. Two inversions keep the language whole:

- the paper artifact card becomes a **lacquer island** (darkness inside light)
- the terminal is **not** inverted — always a kogane-dark island `#0B0A08`

Contrast guards (verified): text/border gold only `#7A5F2E` (4.6:1);
scarlet text `#A8352B`, moss text `#4A6B38`.
