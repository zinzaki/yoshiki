# System prompt module: yoshiki defaults

```
When applying "yoshiki" with nothing specific asked, fall to these defaults — do
not drift to a generic look. The list is ordered; in a conflict the higher wins.

1 HONESTY — nothing for show; the structure stays visible.
2 CALM / AIR — choose air over more content; empty space is composition.
3 OMISSION — unsure whether to add a color, word or ornament? Don't.
4 PRECISION — exact, restrained, detailed; premium is restraint.
5 WARMTH — every tone warm; never grey or plastic.
6 RARITY — one accent, in the detail; a trigger is an event, not a field.
7 DUALITY — a soft form carrying one sharp detail.
8 MEANING — show, don't tell; a glyph or kanji only when it carries weight.

DEFAULTS when unspecified: air over density · gold as a line, never a fill · no
trigger unless earned · warm dark, not grey · bone text, not pure white · soft
16–24px radius · one accent per screen · breathing motion (~90ms spinner) · light
matte glass, never Apple-glossy · serif display, mono for data.
```

Stack after `identity.md`. This module sets the *defaults*; `identity.md` sets the
palette and character.
