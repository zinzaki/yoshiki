01
the one law

Colour earns its
power by being rare.

A signal used everywhere is dead. So a screen is almost all quiet tone, and the rare strike of colour lands like a seal — chosen by meaning, never by taste.

70 · tone20 · bone7 · gold3
tone — lacquer carries the screen bone — text & surfaces gold — edges & links scarlet — the irreversible

Two triggers only: scarlet 緋 for the irreversible, moss 森 for life & success. The rest is tone.
