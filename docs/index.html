01 canon · principles

Principles

The ordered defaults — what wins when nothing is specified. When two choices are both fine, the higher rung wins. The order is the tool.

0
Honesty — nothing for show; the structure stays visible.in doubt: strip back, don't decorate.
1
Calm · air — emptiness with weight; strength in the quiet.in doubt: choose air over more content.
2
Omission — simplicity by removing, not adding.in doubt whether to add: don't.
3
Precision — premium is restraint; exact to the detail.in doubt: exact over loose.
4
Warmth — every tone warm; never grey or plastic.in doubt on a neutral: shift it warm.
5
Rarity = power — one accent, in the detail; the bloom is an event.in doubt about a second accent: don't.
6
Duality — a soft form carrying one sharp detail.in doubt: add one sharp detail, no more.
7
Meaning > ornament — show, don't tell.in doubt: keep only what carries meaning.

defaults — if unspecified

densityair — generous margins, one accent per screen
accentgold as a line / edge / glyph — never a fill
triggernone — live in tone until one is earned
geometrysoft 14–24px radius, optionally one sharp corner
motiona breathing tempo; spinner ~90ms; no dizzy whirl
glass · effectlight, matte, by place — never Apple-glossy
typeserif for display · mono for data & code

04 canon · lexicon

Glyphs

Marks that replace words — one meaning per glyph, applied consistently. See, don't read. Box-drawing and braille are safest: terminals draw them perfectly.

05 canon · lexicon

Frames

Soft rounded capsules for calm content; one sharp corner inside the soft; the deliberate "break"; hard double-line spines for dense data. Soft for words, hard for numbers.

08 canon · motion

Motion

Loading the dot-matrix way — density as meaning. A spinner lives at ~90ms; smoothness belongs to progress, not spinners. One role-colour per run; never a heavy block-slab.

10 canon · effects

Effects

For web surfaces only — tasteful, warm-tinted, never Apple-glossy. One per surface; each honours prefers-reduced-motion and prefers-reduced-transparency.

✦
Frosted
Backdrop-blur + a specular edge + warm tint + fine grain — not flat white.
◈
Spotlight
A soft gold light follows the pointer across the surface.
◆
Tilt & grain
A small 3D lean from the pointer; a static warm grain underneath it all.
彼
The one trigger · 彼岸花

Daring lives in the detail, not the mass. Scarlet is the seal, the focus ring, the irreversible — one per screen, warm and noble, never neon. This is the only place red fills the frame.
