I · the idea
the one law

Color earns its power
by being rare.

A signal used everywhere is dead. So a screen is almost all quiet tone — and the rare strike of color lands like a seal. By meaning, never by taste.

70 · tone20 · bone7 · gold3
彼
the trigger · 彼岸花

One bloom per screen.

Scarlet is the seal, the focus ring, the irreversible — warm and noble, never neon. Its quiet pair is moss, for life and success. Two triggers; the rest is tone.
