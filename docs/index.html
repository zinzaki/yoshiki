canon · principles

Principles

The defaults — what wins when nothing is specified. An ordered ladder: when two choices are both fine, the higher one wins.

0
Honesty — nothing for show; the structure stays visible.in doubt: strip back, don't decorate.
1
Calm · air — emptiness with weight; strength in the quiet.in doubt: choose air over more content.
2
Omission — simplicity by removing, not adding.in doubt whether to add: don't.
3
Precision — premium is restraint; exact to the detail.in doubt: exact over loose.
4
Warmth — every tone warm; never grey or plastic.in doubt on a neutral: shift it warm.
5
Rarity = power — one accent, in the detail; the bloom is an event.in doubt about a second accent: don't.
6
Duality — a soft form carrying one sharp detail.in doubt: add one sharp detail, no more.
7
Meaning > ornament — show, don't tell.in doubt: keep only what carries meaning.

Effects

Glass, refraction and a custom cursor — by place, tasteful, never Apple-glossy. Frosted, warm-tinted, with a specular edge; the prism uses real SVG refraction (Chrome), with a blur fallback.
