SEC.01

Philosophy金継ぎ

Five laws. Everything else on this page is their consequence.

継

Kintsugi

The crack becomes the most valuable place of the vessel. Darkness is the lacquer, gold is the seam, the lily is the memory.

箔

Gold is gilding

No solid-gold watch — but a gilded edge is precious. Gold lives only as line, frame, glyph, thin stroke. Never a filled slab. A primary button is a gold-framed surface, not a gold rectangle.

稀

Rarity = power

A signal's strength is inverse to its frequency. Scarlet fires once per screen — the body reads it before the mind. Paint everything with it and it dies.

極

Two poles, one hand

Hard technical detail — sharp marks, dense trees, HUD annotation — set inside calm, rounded, noble surfaces. Neither pole wins.

温

Warm darkness

No neutral grey exists. Every dark tone carries the urushi undertone; information lives in tone, never in blue. Worlds nest — they do not collide.
